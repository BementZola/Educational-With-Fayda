Frequently Asked Questions

What is my Fayda FIN Number?

Your Fayda FIN Number is your unique identification number provided by the institution for logging into this portal.

I forgot my Fayda FIN Number. What should I do?

Please contact IT support or the administrative office directly to recover your Fayda FIN Number.

My Fayda FIN Number is not working. What's wrong?

Check if you entered it correctly. Your account might be inactive, or there could be a temporary system issue. If it persists, contact IT support.

How do I log in as a student?

Enter your assigned Fayda FIN Number in the login field and click "Login."

I'm a new teacher and don't have my Fayda FIN Number. How do I get it?

New teachers receive their Fayda FIN Number during onboarding or from the administrative department. Contact your department or HR if you haven't received it.
